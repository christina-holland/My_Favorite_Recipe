Recipe Directions

Prep time: 10 minutes, Bake time: 60 minutes

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease a 13x9 glass baking pan with butter.
  3. Melt one stick of butter and one cup of milk in a pot on the stovetop.
  4. Add two cans of cream of chicken soup when butter is melted.
  5. Pour the mixture over a bag of loose hashbrowns in the prepared glass baking pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the casserole.
  8. Allow 5-10 minutes to cool.

Ingredient Calories per Serving

Milk 10
